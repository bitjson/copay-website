We live on bitcoin. This is our wallet.

Copay, the open source, hierarchical-deterministic multisignature bitcoin wallet from BitPay.


At BitPay, we've always held ourselves to a higher standard for Bitcoin security. We've seen account services and exchanges fail bitcoin users time and time again, so we decided to make a bitcoin wallet we would trust with our own company funds.
Many of our own team members live on bitcoin, so we knew we needed to make a true wallet that would work for full-time users. With that in mind, we set out to bring the security and shareability of multisignature wallets together in a flexible finance tool for individuals, groups, and companies.
From Copay's earliest days, we've been 100% open source, working with an international community of developers to build a wallet users could control – and secure – themselves. What started as our own do-it-yourself solution is now out of beta, and we're ready to share it with the world.
